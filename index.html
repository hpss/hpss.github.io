HPSS (High Performance, Scalable systems, and Software) Lab

We enjoy practical systems with focuses on high performance, scalability, and reliability. In particular, we are interested in the broad area of systems software including storage devices, memory, and operating systems. Our research contributions have been published in top-tier systems conferences and journals. If you are interested in building and breaking systems with us, please contact Dr. with your CV.
